UTR's Annotations

Track # Title Annotations
1 GOSSIP "This was one that really needs 'the right musician' to work.
It's classic Måneskin, but from the idea of the 'culture of perfection'.
'[GOSSIP] is the superficial badmouthing and devaluing of someone who doesn't look right.'
Fitting for how much pressure I faced in bands and groups like Melatonin."
2 ugly "This represents the 'obsessed' section of the set.
More on the darker side, similar to 'READ YOUR DIARY', which follows this track.
It shows how disillusioned people could become."
3 READ YOUR DIARY "It's a fictional toxic relationship, and emotional addiction that's obviously harmful.
They may appear harmless, but becomes toxic as we follow the mania they put forth.
It's a strange perspective. It's shows how toxicity and mania disillusions people."
4 Love From The Other Side "This shows the ulterior motive of a pair of lovers.
It's dark and manic in a way, but also light and tricky."
5 She Hates Me "It's a relationship that's not working.
Perspective really matters in this case."
6 Where Do I Sign? "It's a song that doesn't really align with the rest.
It's the idea of a 'want' growing into something darker.
No more pain, but becoming numb and manic in a way."
7 ALL CAPS "A relationship that's too far gone, and the anger of realizing 'this isn't working anymore'."
8 WE MADE PLANS & GOD LAUGHED "Death in the form of a relationship and reality. It's that duality that's strange."
9 Sober "There's a bit of a theme in this. The idea of losing or ending connections with people.
More uptempo and 'punk-like', which plays with the theme directly, rather than implying."
10 headstrong gunner "A depressive look at falling in and out of love."
11 DON'T WANNA SLEEP "It's a detailed look at the need to escape reality. From thoughts and fears.
'To sleep is to be at peace with yourself, and in this song, this feels almost impossible to achieve.'
It's aggressive and wild, manic and dancey in a way."
12 cherry cigarettes "A 'fuck you' to those who talk about me behind my back.
The 'all my exes look the same, but none of them forget my name.' screams the idea of this.
It's uptempo and aggressive. More on the side of 'GOSSIP' than the rest."
13 Le parole lontane "It's rare I choose clear balladry, but whenever I do, I sing my heart out.
This is the best song which fits this idea. It's a song to sing a heart to someone.
It's the idea of pain and anguish. Of missing the person you love and want."
14 Loved You A Little "It's a more bold 'fuck you', in a way of 'I never liked you anyway'.
'If I was the fool, then what does that make you?' is one of the most direct ways to say 'What was I to you?'"
15 LA FINE "More aggressive and angry. It recalls concern from a cultural and societal POV.
'It speaks of worries and anxiety that could arise from certain political conditions,
that affect our daily life through a gradual and slow erosion of civil rights.'
This is one where I could scream the lyrics every time. It's one that makes me angry.
This is the point where I truly 'Unleash The RUSH', and bring forth all of my bottled up rage.
It's rage that comes from many events: both local and international.
'E se non mi amerà nessuno sarà lo stesso' (And if no one loves me, it doesn't matter)."
16 IL DONO DELLA VITA "It's an exaltation of joy, a hymn to the beautiful and simple things in life.
'Like getting results from working on your passions and making yourself and family proud.'
It's slower than many other songs. This is very much a lyrical, ballad-esque track."
17 Happier Than Ever "This is one where I could get all of my screams out.
It's very therapeutic and quiet at the start, and becomes more aggressive near the end.
It's the turning point in the set, a point when anger would lead to calm and catharsis."
18 Male Fantasy "Nothing should end on an angry note. This is where my scars start to heal."
19 THE LONELIEST "This is how I wanted the set to end: a 90's Power Ballad.
It's a fusion of a love letter, farewell and a will.
It's when you know something beautiful is reaching its terminus.
This helped overcome feelings of pain, missing someone and loneliness.
Nostalgia and grief fought through a cathartic cry of love. It's when my scars truly heal.
It's something you'd hear someone cry out to their lover in the pouring rain. It's tearful and cathartic.
The final tearful 'Tonight is gonna be the loneliest' brings a denouement not seen often in a 90's ballad."
